Food Market App
FoodMarket - your partner for culinary adventures! Ever felt overwhelmed by the endless choices in the kitchen? Say no more! With FoodMarket, you can satisfy your cravings while cooking healthy and delicious meals. Your culinary journey begins here, from selecting the freshest ingredients to discovering recipes.

This application was created to satisfy those cravings and was designed using a five-step methodology:
1. Define its Focus and Perspective: I started by organizing my ideas with Post-it notes, whether it’s meal planning, recipe sharing, or ingredient sourcing, FoodMarket is adapted to meet the user’s needs.
2. Explore Example Solutions: I transformed those ideas into wireframes and sketches, ensuring that every detail shines from layout to functionality.
3. Socialize my Ideas: I shared my ideas with others to receive feedback and improve the app. Guiding our discussions through Post-it notes with arguments, while stickers added a touch of fun to the review process. Finally, I refined and elevated FoodMarket to a new level!
4. y 5. Prototyping and testing: Using Figma, I created prototypes that brought FoodMarket to life! From interfaces to seamless interactions, I ensured that every click feels like a culinary experience.
